# Step 02 - Guardrails and Compliance

## Ensuring Safe and Honest Recommendations

Trip recommendations must be safe (no routes through areas with travel warnings), honest (disclose extra costs like insurance and tolls upfront), and appropriate (don't recommend a 2-seat sports car to a family of 5). The system enforces these rules regardless of what the LLM generates.

In this step, you'll implement workflow-level guardrails that validate agent output against business rules, rewrite inappropriate suggestions, and generate audit trails for compliance.
